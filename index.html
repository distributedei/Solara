Why Solara

Built for the Storm

We're not selling solar panels. We're selling peace of mind — designed by Floridians who've been through it.

🌀

Hurricane-Rated to 170mph

Every mount is PE-stamped for Florida wind zones. Our aluminum frames are tested beyond Cat 5 standards. Your panels stay put when the storm hits.

🔌

Zero Permits. Zero Hassle.

Our kits are self-contained off-grid battery systems. No utility interconnection, no building permits, no HOA electrical approvals needed. Plug in and go.

🏭

Assembled in Stuart, FL

Every kit is assembled by hand in our Stuart facility. Local jobs, local quality control, and next-day delivery anywhere on the Treasure Coast.

🔋

LFP Battery Technology

Lithium Iron Phosphate batteries last 10+ years, don't overheat in Florida summers, and are the safest chemistry available. No thermal runaway risk.

How It Works

Power in 15 Minutes

1

Call Us or Order Online

Call 772-521-1871 to talk to our team. We'll help you choose the right kit for your home, condo, or RV. Same-day shipping on in-stock kits.

2

Unbox & Mount

Every kit comes pre-assembled with color-coded connectors. Mount the panel on your balcony, patio, or yard using our hurricane-rated hardware. No tools beyond a wrench.

3

Plug In & Power Up

Connect the panel to the battery unit. That's it. The system starts charging immediately. Download the app to monitor your power in real-time.

4

Sleep Easy During Hurricane Season

When the grid goes down, your Solara keeps your essentials running. Phones charged, lights on, fridge cold. No gas, no fumes, no noise. Just the sun.
